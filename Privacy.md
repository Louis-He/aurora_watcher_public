# Aurora Watcher – Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2025-11-28**

Aurora Watcher respects your privacy. This app does **not** collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Aurora Watcher does not collect:

- Personal data (name, email, address, etc.)
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Cookies
- Device identifiers
- Any other data that can be used to identify you

## How the App Works

Aurora Watcher processes all aurora visibility data, geomagnetic information, and map displays **locally on your device** or retrieves publicly available space-weather data from our server **without attaching any user information**.

No data is ever stored on our servers, or shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Aurora Watcher does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

## Children’s Privacy

Because Aurora Watcher collects **no data**, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If a future update requires data collection, we will update this policy and notify users before changes take effect.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the public repository.
